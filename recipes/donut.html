How to Make Donuts

Making donuts at home is fun and rewarding! Below you'll find a simple ingredient list and step-by-step instructions to make classic fried donuts.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar or glaze (optional)

Steps for Making Donuts

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, butter, and egg. Combine with dry ingredients to form a dough.
  3. Roll out dough and cut into donut shapes.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry donuts until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Drain on paper towels. Dust with powdered sugar or dip in glaze if desired.
  6. Enjoy your homemade donuts!
