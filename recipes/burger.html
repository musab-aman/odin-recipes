How to Make a Burger

Making a homemade burger is easy and delicious! Here are the ingredients and steps to make a classic beef burger at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo

Steps for Making a Burger

  1. Divide ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape into patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes per side, adding cheese if desired.
  3. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  4. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and your favorite sauces.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade burgers!
